Biography

A Life Illuminated by Divine Grace

Born in Bhusawal, Maharashtra, Shri Balkrishna Maharaj arrived bearing extraordinary signs of spiritual destiny — a yogic posture identical to Shri Shankar Maharaj and the sacred Astha Vakra, symbolizing transcendence of physical limitations.

His spiritual journey began at an impossibly early age — guiding souls from the age of one and mastering Tratak Sadhana by eight. He possesses profound knowledge of scriptures, sciences, technology, and multiple languages without formal education — attributed to divine grace and past-life wisdom.

Birth Born with the sacred Astha Vakra (eight bends) in Bhusawal, Maharashtra.
2 months First visit to Shri Shankar Baba Maharaj Math at Dhankawadi, Pune.
Age 8 Mastered Tratak Sadhana — concentrated gazing for spiritual awakening.
1979 Began 45+ years of continuous service to seekers across the globe.
